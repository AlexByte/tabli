Tabli 1.1.1
Aug. 23, 2018
Fixes / Features:
  • Tabs are now rendered as hyperlinks. This means all of Chrome's context menu actions for hyperlinks are available in Tabli and behave as expected.
  • You can now re-open saved tabs individually. If you expand a closed, saved window and click on an individual closed tab, Tabli will re-open the saved window with just that tab open. Clicking on the window header (as opposed to a tab) will re-open all previously open tabs for that window.
...plus a few other internal upgrades and minor bug fixes.
